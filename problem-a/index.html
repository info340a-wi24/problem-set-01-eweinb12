Garfield the Cat

Garfield is an orange and black cartoon cat who was originally part of a comic strip. The comic strip has since been turned into books, cartoons, and movies. Garfield lives with his owner Jon and a dog named Odie. Garfield is known for being very lazy, hating mondays, and loving lasagna. He also loves his stuffed bear pookie.

Reasons I admire Garfield

  • Garfield sleeps all day. I admire his commitment to doing nothing
  • Garfield lives completely rent and bill free. I aspire to do the same
  • Garfield eats seemingly limitless amounts of lasagna. As someone who is allergic to dairy, I admire his ability to eat dairy with no reaction
